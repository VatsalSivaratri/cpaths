The Problem

Overview

Every year, heart and lung diseases take the lives of millions around the globe. Diseases like Cardiovascular Disease (CVD), COPD, and Asthma affect more than a billion people worldwide, reducing their quality of life and increasing their risk for premature death. The burden of these illnesses extends beyond the individual, affecting families and healthcare systems.

Current Solutions

Current methods of managing these diseases involve tests performed at doctor’s offices and a review of the patient’s medical history. Unfortunately, this approach is plagued with flaws. They address the condition too late in its progression for easy treatment plans. On top of this, doctor’s lack historical information of a patient’s condition in their home setting, rendering them unable to offer sufficient and specific treatment plans.

Our Innovation

We devloped a curated, 5-step patient pathway that uses ECG, PPG, and SpO2 sensors to screen, analyze, and optimize treatments for patient’s with heart and lung conditions. Our pathway reflects the demands of a wide-range of different heart and lung conditions with a close, one-fits-all framework, while caring for indviidual needs.

Hardware Drug Delivery System

Medication Adherence and Management are key bottlenecks in the field of medicine, with average adherence to medications for chronic conditions being at only 50%. In conjunction with algorithms to determine best treatment windows, we build a hardware drug delivery system to administer prescribed concentrations from doctor's instructions at the optimal times, while following treatent constraints to prevent toxicity.
